DMI Engineering College was founded in 2008 under the visionary leadership of Father J.E.Arul Raj, with the guidance of Principal Dr.A.Albert Raj.This institution has been dedicated to fostering a comprehensive educational environment that emphasizes innovation and excellence in engineering disciplines. With a commitment to high quality education and the holistic development of students, DMI Engineering College strives to equip future engineers with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their careers and contibute meaningfully to society.
